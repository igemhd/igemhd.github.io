Welcome to the front page of the iGEM Team Heidelberg 2020!


Are you a student wondering whether iGEM is right for you? Then you have come to the right place.

We are looking for motivated students of all scientific disciplines and beyond, and let us tell you, iGEM is – indeed – very much right for you.

We are a studend-led team of biologists, mathematicians and physicists (and some friendly PhD students) competing in iGEM – the international Genetically Engineered Machine competition

The iGEM competition unites the fields of biology, mathematics and physics in setting up and going through massively interdisciplinary projects, centered on synthetic biology. These range from straight up protein engineering to setting up complete systems involving software, cells and microfluidic hardware.

Heidelberg looks back on a very long and successful history of participating in iGEM. Over the years, Heidelberg iGEM Teams have won the Grand Prize multiple times, not to mention the many special and track prizes won by Heidelberg over the years.

Now, we want to follow in their footsteps and shape iGEM Heidelberg anew, more interdisciplinary than ever before.


And for that, we need your help!
